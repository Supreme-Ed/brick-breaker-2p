Brick Breaker

Controls:

Player 1 (Bottom):

  • Keyboard: Left/Right Arrows (Move), Space (Power-up)
  • Mouse (Single Player): Move Mouse (Move), Click (Power-up)
  • Touch (Single Player): Touch & Drag lower screen (Move), Tap lower screen (Power-up)

Player 2 (Top):

  • Keyboard: A/D Keys (Move), S (Power-up)
  • Touch (Two Players): Touch & Drag upper screen (Move), Tap upper screen (Power-up)

General:

  • Pause/Resume: 'P' Key or Pause/Resume Button
  • Return to Menu: ESC Key or Return to Menu Button
  • Restart: Restart Game Button
